There are infinite questions generated in our mind when we talk about our LONELINESS IN SPACE.

To find out a solution, one of our great scientists named 'Dr. Frank Drake' proposed an equation in Green Bank Facility in 1961 named "Drake's Equation".

This equation simply estimates the number of alien civilizations that are present in our Galaxy with whom we can communicate.

The results come out to be 100,000,000 living civilizations are hiding in our Galaxy itself.

Then where are they?

This could be possible that they may see us as we were millions of years ago.
